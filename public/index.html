What is Dint?

We’ve built a KYC platform
for crypto-investors

A one-stop shop

With thousands upon thousands of crypto projects, investors don't want to leave their personal data all over the place. And when you run your own ICO, you want to make sure your investors feel that their private information is in good hands. Dint is here to make that possible. With a simple KYC process, you can now easily safeguard the KYC-information of your investors, while ensuring that people who take part in your ICO are real and verified.

What is Dint?

Dint is a unique KYC platform for investors, ICOs and projects in the crypto space. It allows ICOs a way to easily manage their KYC-requirements. At the same time, Dint allows investors to safely store their KYC-data, without their passport having to go to some shady project or database.

Why should I use Dint as an ICO?

You should start using Dint because it allows you to outsource the KYC process, and provide additional credibility and transparency to your investors. Simply put, there's only upsides!

Why should I use Dint as an investor?

You should use Dint because it allows you to easily invest in multiple ICOs, without having to do the KYC verification process every time. In this way, we make your life easier.

Where is Dint based?

Dint is a team of committed individuals with a keen interest in the crypto space, all based in the beautiful Netherlands.
