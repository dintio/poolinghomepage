What is Dint?

We’ve built a KYC platform
for ICO pools

A one-stop shop

With crypto projects focusing more and more on private-sales, it's difficult for a pool to stand out from the crowd. Dint is here to change that. With a simple KYC process, pools can easily show third-parties that their investors are real and verified. This makes pools more reliable and trustworthy, improving their relationships with ICOs and making it easier to invest in high-profile projects.

Why Dint

Data Safely Secured

Regulatory Compliance

Our KYC service ensures you can comply with KYC regulation. It's as easy as that.

One KYC — Multiple Investments

Dint allows investors to use the same KYC process for different investments.
